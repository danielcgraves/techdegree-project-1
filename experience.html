Previous Work

As mentioned in the home page, I have been one of the top guitar teachers at the On Track Music Guitar School for nearly 10 years now. Through the years I have had other side jobs as well! I have been a barista, an Amazon delivery driver, a rideshare driver, and more!

Education

I graduated from Intermountain Christian High School in 2011. From there I went directly into the music program at Salt Lake Community College. I pursued music for a couple of years, including becoming my decision to become a guitar teacher and then I decided to switch things up and go into the Business Management program as well. Although I never got my degree in either of these programs I gained a lot of experience and learned a lot of valuable information and life lessons.

Now I am currently enrolled in the Front End Web Developer Techdegree program with Team Treehouse. I have dabbled with HTML and CSS in the past but now I want to get serious and hone a new skillset. I plan to complete this Front End Web Developer certificate and then move forward into the Full Stack JavaScript program. These are my current ambitions but I know that my learning won't stop there. I'm so excited to see where this journey will take me!
